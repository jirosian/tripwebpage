Scuba Diving

Phangan Island has some of the most beautiful fish in the world. There are shallow caves and caverns to explore and divers will can encounter grouper, anemonefish, rabbitfish, damselfish, butterflyfish, snappers, stingrays, barracudas, and much more!

Beaches

Ishigaki Island has both sandy beaches and rocky beaches covered in small pieces of bleached coral. At the beaches you can enjoy swimming, snorkeling, and diving, or just lay in the sun.
