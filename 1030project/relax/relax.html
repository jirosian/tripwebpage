#1 Ishigaki Island

For people who want to enjoy both nature and city

 Ishigaki Island has incredibly beautiful sea, white sands, and colourful coral leaves. Most of the 48000 people that make up the island’s population reside in Ishigaki City.

 It is very quiet and fewer people visit there than other famous locations such as Okinawa city. People in Ishigaki Island are very calm and friendly so you can easily associate with them.

Things you can do

  What best thing to do in resort is not to do anything. You can just relax at the beach listening the sound of waves, drinking delicious tropical juice and taking a nap as much as you want.

  You can also hung out in the sea and enjoy snorkelling. Going to a small concert to listen to the sound of traditional instrument in Okinawa called minser allows you to spend relaxing time.

Food/Tourist attractions

Things you can eat

  Ishigaki island is in the group of Okinawa so you can have Okinawan foods and tropical drink. sea grapes tastes slightly salty with an oceanic freshness. Orion beer is also famous drink in there. It taste lighter than other beer and so it can be good with any kind of foods. You can be apart from your busy daily life with delicious foods and beer in a cozy restaurant.

Places you should visit

Fusaki Beach is located in west side of the island. It is very popular for couples and family. It is also famous for a sunset spot and so many people come there at sunset. There is also limestone cave in Ishigaki island. It is very mysterious and beautiful with a lot of light. It is a great place for instagram for sure.

#2 Lankayan Island

For people who are interested in animal ecology.

Habitats for a lof of animals

  Lankayan island is a small tropical coral resort island in the Sulu sea. The ocean is very transparent and there are more than 10 places for scuba diving. Since it is such a small island, you can see beautiful bright stars fill the dark spaces in the sky at night, which is the moment that you cannot experience in cities.

Things we can do

 The main thing you can do is scuba diving. Nutrients from the tropical rainforests of Borneo’s mainland flow into the sea, where fish grow in size to be surprised.

In the month of June to September, you might have a chance to see sea turtles since they come to the island for spawning. There is also a tour that you can meet orang-utans so you can heal yourself with adorable and innocent animals.

Food/Tourist attractions

Things you can eat

There are many type of delicious foods in Malaysia. In Malaysia, Indian food plays a major part in the diverse spectrum of the food there. The food called banana leaf is originated in south Indian and often served vegetarian, but you can also normally order sides of meat too. It looks very exotic and tropical style so you can also visually enjoy it.

Places you should visit

There is a place called orang-utan centre. It established as a protected area in natural primeval forest as a rehabilitation facility to protect orang-utans that lost their habitat during deforestation and return to forest life again. You can visit there and observe them. Most young orang-utans are couples and one of them comes with her shoulders crossed, which is so adore .
